Anordica corporate text

Anordica AB is a modern Swedish industrial material and equipment supplying company located in the south of Sweden. Founded in 1996, we are one of the largest wire suppliers in Scandinavia and have formed close relationships with suppliers and customers throughout Scandinavia and Europe. Over the years we have acquired experience and knowledge about the products and applications, logistical and financial solutions as well as risk management in the steel business.

Anordica’s product range includes metal wire, ropes, gabions, chains, netting, and similar products used in industries such as construction, forest, recycling and others.
